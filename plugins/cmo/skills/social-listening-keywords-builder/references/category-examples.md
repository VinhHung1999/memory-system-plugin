## Common category mistakes

### Brand category
- ❌ Only tracking exact name, missing domain/handle/lowercase
- ✅ Include: exact, lowercase, spaced, domain, social handle, any common URL slugs

### Misspellings
- ❌ Generating 50 nonsense variants
- ✅ Top 5-8 actual likely typos based on keyboard layout + phonetic rules

### People
- ❌ Tracking all employees
- ✅ Only public-facing: founder, CEO, head of PR, thought-leader employees with big followings

### Industry buzzwords
- ❌ "marketing" (too broad, 1000s of unrelated mentions)
- ✅ 2-3 word phrases that narrow to your niche

### Hashtags
- ❌ Tracking only #YourBrand (low volume)
- ✅ Mix of branded (low volume, high relevance) + community (high volume, must filter)

### Competitors
- ❌ 15 competitors (signal drowns in noise)
- ✅ 3-5 direct rivals only
