# Quarterly CMO ⇄ CEO ⇄ CFO Review — ${QUARTER}

**Attendees:** CMO (owner), CEO, CFO
**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Cadence:** Quarterly (90 days)
**Pre-read:** 1-page intelligence brief sent 48h prior

## Agenda

### 1. Last Quarter Outcomes (10 min) — CMO leads
- ${OUTCOME_1}: variance vs forecast (target: <10 percent)
- ${OUTCOME_2}
- Top 1 win + top 1 miss + root cause of miss

### 2. Customer + Market Signals (10 min) — CMO leads
- 3 customer signals exec team should know
- 1 competitive signal
- 1 macro/segment shift

### 3. Strategic Bets — Next Quarter (15 min) — CEO leads
- Confirm Top 3 bets aligned with company strategy
- Marketing investment thesis per bet (NOT spend bucket)
- Kill criteria + decision gates

### 4. Budget + Forecast Review (5 min) — CFO leads
- Forecast accuracy
- Reallocation requests with payback periods
- One concern CFO has on marketing capital efficiency

### 5. Open Risks + Asks (5 min) — Round-robin
- 1 ask per leader from the other two

## Outputs (recorded)
- Top 3 marketing bets approved
- Reallocation decisions
- Open risks logged

## Anti-patterns (DO NOT)
- Status update without decisions
- Tactical campaign review
- Marketing presenting alone with CEO/CFO observing

## Success indicator
- All 3 attendees can articulate the top 3 marketing bets one week later WITHOUT looking
