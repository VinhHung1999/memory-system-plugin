# Subject Line Formulas

20 proven patterns organized by email goal. Each includes a template + example.

## Core rules (Course 4 M3 m3_04)

1. **Clarity > catchiness** — answer "what are you offering?" in the subject
2. **30-60 chars** — mobile preview cuts at ~50
3. **Personalization** — include first name when relevant (`{FNAME}` or `{{first_name}}`)
4. **1 emoji max** — brand-fit only; test per list
5. **Avoid spam triggers** — FREE!!!, ALL CAPS, $$$, "act now"
6. **A/B test 2-3 variants** per send

## By goal

### Acquisition (awareness)

1. **The question hook** — `Still using {competitor_or_old_method}?`
   Ex: "Still using 10 tools to manage your Instagram?"

2. **The stat hook** — `{Stat}% of {audience} don't know this`
   Ex: "73% of small businesses skip this SEO step"

3. **The gift-first** — `{Resource} for {audience}, no signup needed`
   Ex: "Free email template library for D2C marketers"

### Welcome (consideration)

4. **Warm intro** — `Welcome, {FNAME}! Here's your first {thing}`
   Ex: "Welcome, Hung! Here's your first template"

5. **Expectation setter** — `What to expect from {brand}`
   Ex: "What to expect from PlantCo emails"

6. **First-action nudge** — `Get started in 2 minutes, {FNAME}`

### Newsletter (maintenance)

7. **Date/issue** — `The {brand} Weekly: {topic}`
   Ex: "The PlantCo Weekly: 3 cacti for low light"

8. **Curated list** — `This week's top {N} {thing}`
   Ex: "This week's top 5 wholesale finds"

9. **Question to reader** — `{FNAME}, how do you handle {pain_point}?`

### Promotional (purchase)

10. **Direct offer** — `{%} off {category} this weekend only`
    Ex: "30% off all pots this weekend only"

11. **Scarcity** — `{N} left: {product} (ends {deadline})`
    Ex: "12 left: Red terracotta pot (ends Sunday)"

12. **Bundle/savings** — `Buy {X}, get {Y} — limited time`
    Ex: "Buy 2 cacti, get a free pot — this week"

13. **New launch** — `Just dropped: {product}`
    Ex: "Just dropped: The winter collection"

### Retention (loyalty/win-back)

14. **Missing you** — `{FNAME}, we've missed you`
    Simple and effective. Open rates often highest of any retention pattern.

15. **Personalized offer** — `A {%} off just for you, {FNAME}`

16. **Replenishment** — `Time to reorder your {product}?`
    Ex: "Time to reorder your cactus food?"

17. **Last chance** — `{FNAME}, this is goodbye unless…`
    Sunset flow — last email before removing from list.

### Universal patterns (work across types)

18. **Question** — `Are you {X}?` — engages curiosity
    Ex: "Are you overpaying for shipping?"

19. **Numbered list promise** — `{N} {things} that {outcome}`
    Ex: "5 tools that cut my ad spend 40%"

20. **Teaser** — `This {surprising thing} changed my {area}`
    Ex: "This one change doubled our open rates"

## Don't

- **FREE!!!! LIMITED TIME!!!** — spam trigger
- **Sent via {ESP}** — kills trust
- **{empty subject}** — auto-flagged
- **Subject lines over 78 chars** — truncated in most clients
- **Re: / Fwd: fake replies** — unethical, reported as spam

## A/B test recommendations

For every send, test:
- **Variant A**: your best instinct, matching past winners
- **Variant B**: a clarity variant (strip all cleverness, just state the offer)
- **Variant C** (optional): a personalization variant (add `{FNAME}`)

Send to 10% of list first (5% A, 5% B), wait 2-6 hours, pick winner by open rate, send winner to remaining 90%.
