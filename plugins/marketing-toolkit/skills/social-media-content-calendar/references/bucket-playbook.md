# Bucket Playbook — 20 prompt patterns per bucket

Fill these patterns with brand specifics when drafting posts.

## Educational (30% default)

1. **List** — "3 ways to {goal}" / "5 mistakes when {task}"
2. **Myth-bust** — "{Common belief} is wrong. Here's why..."
3. **Tutorial** — "How to {specific task} in {time}"
4. **Data insight** — "We analyzed {N} {items}. Here's what we found."
5. **Definition** — "What is {concept}? (And why it matters)"
6. **Comparison** — "{A} vs {B}: when to use each"
7. **Timeline** — "The evolution of {topic} in 5 moments"
8. **Checklist** — "Before you {action}, check these {N} things"
9. **Framework** — "The {N}-step process for {outcome}"
10. **Case study** — "How {brand/person} achieved {outcome}"
11. **FAQ** — "Top question we get: {Q}. Answer: ..."
12. **Tool** — "We built/use this {tool} for {job}. Here's a preview."

## Entertaining (25% default)

1. **BTS (behind-the-scenes)** — Office, team, process photos
2. **Meme** — Relatable industry humor (only if brand voice fits)
3. **Outtake** — "The blooper reel from our latest shoot"
4. **Team intro** — "Meet {name}, our {role}. Fun fact: ..."
5. **Office pet** — Dog/cat content always wins
6. **Joke** — Industry-specific wordplay
7. **This or that** — Visual A/B ("Cactus or fern?" "Morning or evening?")
8. **TGIF / Monday mood** — Casual rhythm markers
9. **Celebration** — Team milestone, cake photos, office party
10. **Reaction** — Team reacting to news/trend
11. **Trend jack** — Participate in current meme carefully
12. **Customer-sent photos** — "Best pictures you've sent us this month"

## Inspirational (20% default)

1. **Customer success** — "{Customer} achieved {outcome} using {product}"
2. **Founder story** — Origin moments, why the brand exists
3. **Milestone** — "Today we hit {number}. Here's what got us here."
4. **Quote card** — Customer review or industry leader quote
5. **Mission moment** — Why we do what we do
6. **Community highlight** — Feature a user/customer/partner
7. **Transformation** — Before/after customer result
8. **Thank-you post** — Gratitude to customers/team
9. **Values in action** — Example of brand values applied
10. **Contribution** — Charity/nonprofit/cause involvement
11. **Industry pioneer** — Honor someone who shaped the field
12. **Milestone reflection** — "1 year ago today we {accomplishment}"

## Conversational (15% default)

1. **Open question** — "What's your biggest challenge with {topic}?"
2. **Poll** — "This or that?" with 2-4 options
3. **Fill-in-blank** — "I could never give up my ___"
4. **Hot take** — "Unpopular opinion: {controversial view}"
5. **AMA prompt** — "Reply with your {topic} questions"
6. **Tag-a-friend** — "Who needs to hear this?"
7. **Caption contest** — "Caption this" with funny image
8. **Would-you-rather** — Two options about brand topic
9. **Share your {X}** — "Show me your {desk / plant collection / setup}"
10. **Advice request** — "Help us decide: {A} or {B}?"
11. **Survey** — "Tap to vote: {question}"
12. **Experience recall** — "What was your first {experience}?"

## Promotional (10% default, max 20%)

1. **Product launch** — "Introducing {product}. Here's what it does."
2. **Sale** — "{%} off {category} until {deadline}"
3. **Limited edition** — "Only {N} available. First come first served."
4. **Bundle** — "Buy {A}, get {B} free"
5. **Restock** — "Back in stock: {sold-out favorite}"
6. **Pre-order** — "Reserve yours before {launch date}"
7. **Feature highlight** — "You might not know {product} can also..."
8. **Use case** — "3 ways customers use {product}"
9. **Referral** — "Send us a friend, both get {reward}"
10. **Event** — "Free workshop / webinar on {date}"
11. **Collab** — "We teamed up with {brand} on {thing}"
12. **Price drop** — "{Product} is now {new price}"

## Mixing rules

- **Never 2 promos in a row** — kills engagement. Sandwich between educational/conversational.
- **Post 3 non-promos before a promo** — builds goodwill for the ask.
- **Conversational posts amplify others** — schedule a question right after a promo to re-engage the same audience.
- **Entertaining posts need voice fit** — if your brand is B2B formal, "memes" means "polished industry wit" not TikTok randomness.

## When a bucket doesn't fit your brand

- **B2B SaaS** — mostly educational (50%), very little entertaining, moderate promotional. Conversational = LinkedIn polls.
- **E-commerce / DTC** — more promotional OK (up to 25%); heavy visual inspirational; UGC-driven.
- **Nonprofit** — heavy inspirational (40%); educational for awareness; rare promotional (only fundraisers).
- **Creator / personal brand** — all buckets but "promotional" = your content/courses, not products. Conversational is where audience is built.
