# Platform Rules — Frequency, Format, Tone, Hashtags

From Course 3 Module 2 m2_04 "Choose platforms" + m2_07 "Frequency and timing".

## Twitter / X

- **Frequency:** 3-10 posts/day (chronological feed → more is fine)
- **Length:** 280 chars hard limit; aim for ~180 for readability
- **Tone:** Conversational, quick, opinion-friendly
- **Hashtags:** 1-2 max (more = spam signal)
- **Best hours (US):** 8am, 12pm, 5pm, 8pm ET
- **Content strengths:** Real-time reactions, threads, polls, AMAs, news
- **Image specs:** 1200×675 (16:9) or 1200×1200 (1:1)

## Instagram

- **Frequency:** 1-2 grid posts/day + 3-5 stories/day + 1-3 reels/week
- **Length:** Captions 150+ chars (more = more in-feed time)
- **Tone:** Visual-first, aspirational, polished
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 (in caption or first comment; 30 is spam now)
- **Best hours:** 11am, 2pm, 7pm
- **Content strengths:** Visual storytelling, reels, carousels, BTS
- **Image specs:** 1080×1080 (square) or 1080×1350 (portrait) or 1080×1920 (reel/story)

## Facebook

- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts/day
- **Length:** 80-150 chars get best reach (counterintuitive — longer gets seen less)
- **Tone:** Community-oriented, older-skewing
- **Hashtags:** 1-2 (don't help much on FB)
- **Best hours:** 9am, 1pm, 3pm
- **Content strengths:** Group engagement, live video, events, link posts
- **Image specs:** 1200×630

## LinkedIn

- **Frequency:** 3-5 posts/week (NOT every day — dilutes signal)
- **Length:** 1200-2000 chars optimal; use line breaks every 1-2 sentences
- **Tone:** Professional but human; stories > corporate speak
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 focused on industry
- **Best hours:** Tue-Thu, 7-9am or 5-6pm
- **Content strengths:** Career narratives, B2B insights, native articles, document carousels
- **Image specs:** 1200×627 or 1080×1080

## TikTok

- **Frequency:** 1-3 videos/day
- **Length:** 15-60s (sweet spot 21-34s for full watch); up to 10min allowed
- **Tone:** Authentic, trend-aware, low production OK
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 mixing niche + trending
- **Best hours:** 9am, 7pm, 10pm
- **Content strengths:** Trending audio, duets, stitches, tutorials
- **Video specs:** 1080×1920 (9:16 vertical)

## YouTube

- **Frequency:** 1 long-form/week OR 1 short/day (not both casually — pick a lane)
- **Length:**
  - Shorts: <60s vertical
  - Long-form: 8-12 min is algorithm sweet spot for non-tutorial; 15-30 min for tutorial/deep-dive
- **Tone:** Depends on channel — pick one voice and keep it
- **Tags:** 5-15 tags, first tag = primary keyword
- **Best publish:** Thu-Sun, 2-4pm (before evening watch window)
- **Content strengths:** Tutorials, vlogs, reviews, explainers
- **Thumbnail:** 1280×720, bold text + close-up face

## Cross-platform notes

### Don't auto-crosspost
Same post on IG + Twitter + LinkedIn = ignored on all three. Adapt format + length per platform.

### Post time matters more for low-frequency platforms
- Twitter: post whenever (chronological)
- LinkedIn: Tuesday 7am vs Saturday 9pm = massive reach difference
- Instagram: algorithmic, but first-hour engagement sets trajectory

### Hashtag philosophy
- Twitter/FB: hashtags are weak; use sparingly
- Instagram/TikTok: hashtags still help discoverability
- LinkedIn: hashtags increase feed signal slightly

### Time zones
If audience is global, stagger posts across US morning + EU evening + APAC morning. Each post gets 3 shots instead of 1.
